Hello there!

Daniel Schutzsmith is a rare breed – a hybrid of equal parts business, design, coding, and strategy – ready to change the world from the Hudson Valley of New York State.

Over the past 19 years he's had the privilege of working with companies such as Adobe, Greenpeace, The Pixies, MTV, Phish, PBS, They Might Be Giants, TBS, Pepsico, Dave Matthews Band & more.

In his free time he enjoys single malt scotches, inspirational wanderings through the streets of NYC, hiking the hills of the Hudson Valley, and evenings at the drive-in with his wife and three kids.
